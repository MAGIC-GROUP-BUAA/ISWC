Organizers of this competition are:

  • Qingyun Sun is currently an assistant professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering and the Beijing Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Computing at Beihang University, China. She has published over 20 papers on graph learning and social network mining on TPAMI, TKDE, NeurIPS, WWW, AAAI, ICDM, CIKM, COLING, etc. She was the recipient of the Best Paper Award of IEEE IWQoS 2022, Best Paper Honorable Mention Award of CIKM 2022, Best Paper Candidate of ICDM 2021, and the most influential WWW 2021 papers. She was also the recipient of the 2023 ACM China Beijing Doctoral Dissertation Award and the Excellence Award in Pretrained Model-based Community Detection and Fraudulent Group Mining Competition of ICDM 2023. She serves as the session chair of ICDM 2023 and the program committee member of NeurIPS 2023, ICLR 2023, AAAI 2022/2023, IJCAI 2024, WWW 2021/2022/2024, and KDD 2021/2022/2024. She serves as the reviewer of IEEE TKDE, IEEE TNNLS, IEEE TMM, IEEE TCYB, ACM TWEB, PR, NN, JMLC, JCST, and JSC.
  • Jianxin Li is currently a Professor at the School of Computer Science and Engineering and the Beijing Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Computing at Beihang University, China. His research interests include social networks, machine learning, big data, and trustworthy computing. He has published research papers in top-tier journals and conferences, including the TKDE, TDSC, TOIS, TKDD, KDD, WWW, and AAAI. He was the recipient of the Best Paper Award of AAAI 2021 and the Best Paper Award of DependSys 2017. He has won the gold medal in Kaggle IEEE-CIS Fraud Detection, the gold medal in Kaggle TalkingData AdTracking Fraud Detection Challenge, and the first place in the Maven Event Detection Challenge. He serves as the associate editor of IEEE Transactions of Computers, Frontiers of Computer Science, Big Data Mining and Analytics, the leading guest editor for IEEE TKDE, and the editorial member of the Journal of Social Computing and the Journal of Cloud Computing. He also serves as the Program Co-Chair of more than 8 international conferences, including the CPSCom, MWNS, IEEE TrustCom, DSOC 2012, HPCC, iVCE, ISORC, and iThings.
  • Ruijie Wang is currently a postdoctoral research associate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Illinois Urbana-Champaign (UIUC), working with Prof. Tarek F. Abdelzaher. His research interests lie in deep graph learning algorithms for real-world graphs at scale to understand the underlying dynamic patterns and predict future knowledge. He is also generally interested in machine learning and deep learning on graphs, natural language, and time-series data, with applications on social network analysis, knowledge graph, and dynamic systems. He has published several research papers in top-tier conferences including NeurIPS, WWW, ACL, SIGIR, AAAI, CIKM, etc. He has also served as program committee and reviewer for several top-tier journals and conferences including IEEE TKDE, IEEE TNNLS, NeurIPS, ICLR, AAAI, IJCAI, KDD, ACL, EMNLP, etc.
  • Xingcheng Fu is currently an assistant professor in the School of Computer Science and Engineering at Guangxi Normal University. He received his Ph.D. degree from the School of Computer Science and Engineering, Beihang University. He has published over 20 papers on graph geometric deep learning and social network analysis on TKDE, NeurIPS, WWW, AAAI, ICDM, CIKM, ECAI, etc. He was the recipient of the Spotlight Paper of WWW 2023, Best Paper Candidate of ICDM 2021, Best Paper Honorable Mention Award of CIKM 2022. He serves as the reviewer for several top-tier journals and conferences including IEEE TKDE, IEEE TNNLS, ACM TWEB, NeurIPS, ICLR, WWW, AAAI, IJCAI, KDD, etc.
  • Jiayi Luo is currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Beijing Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Computing at Beihang University. His research interests include robust graph representation learning and trustworthy AI.
  • Haonan Yuan is currently a Ph.D. candidate at the Beijing Advanced Innovation Center for Big Data and Brain Computing at Beihang University. His research interests include dynamic graph learning and graph foundation models. He has published several research papers in top-tier conferences including NeurIPS, WWW, AAAI, CIKM, etc.
